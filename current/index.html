What's Going On?

On any given day:

  • 365,000 people are born on earth
  • 1 or 2 meoterites hit the earth's surface
  • the top ten websites generate 7 billion visits
  • 150-200 different kinds of plants, insects, birds and mammals become extinct
  • 93,000 rats will be born in London alone
  • lighting will strike 8.6 million times
  • 142,000 new cars will roll off the conveyer belt

There's a lot going on on our little planet.
