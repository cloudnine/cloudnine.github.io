Spring Time. We're Doing Great.

Author: Yuri Tricys |   Mar 19, 2020  |    personal development

Today I took the train around town and ran some errands. There's an apocalypse. There were a lot of unhappy people in the streets. Rephrase that. Everyone in the street appeared unhappy. I understand. It's a pandemic.

One of my Facebook readers responded to my last post about Julian Assange. Why so serious? What's all this posting about war?

I understand. Doom and gloom subjects weigh our spirits down.

A politician once told me you can't change the world, you can only squeeze it to get what you want out of it. I coughed. I'm progressive. I've nothing against business and capitalism. Make your money. Do your job. I just get motivation and spiritual sustenance from human progress. I do what little I can to help, including posting about Assange.

So this post is dedicated to progress.

Many others and myself believe humanity is progressing. The last time I checked, I was allowed to vote, and so was the woman working at the grocery store till. 100 years ago that would not have been the case. Progress. 😉

The following are from a newsletter some greenies were sending around some years ago. I suppose much of it is probably true.

So that's all for now. If the virus has you down, hang in there and stress less. Things have a way of turning around.
