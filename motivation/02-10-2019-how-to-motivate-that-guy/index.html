How To Motivate That Guy

By Cloud Nine |  Oct 2, 2019  | leadership

Here you have a header

Sample Post Auto Deploy Testy

Whether you are dealing with friends, family, co-workers, or employees, there is always that one guy (or gal) who just isn’t getting anything done. Life has got a grip on them, and most of the nasties out there aren’t helping him out. As a leader your job is not to tell people what to do (that’s a boss, by the way). Leaders help people achieve goals, both their own and those of the organization they are working in, by motivating them to do better for themselves, through example, feedback, and pep talking. So the question should be, not how to replace that guy, but how to motivate him.
